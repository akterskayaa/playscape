We are Radiant IRIS , a creative studio and global ecosystem designing immersive storytelling rituals that awaken collective imagination and spiritual connection.

What It Is

Playscape Portals is a participatory storytelling and ritual platform
designed to help groups practice becoming more connected,
self-aware, and collaborative together.

Using immersive story worlds , biofeedback , AI-generated reflections , and collective ritual , participants move through shared experiences that help reconnect:

Reconnect
Mind with Body

Biofeedback and embodied practices bring the body's wisdom back into the room — as a source of truth, not distraction.

Reconnect
Individuals with Communities

Collective ritual and participatory story rebuild the trust and relational tissue that isolation and polarization erode.

Reconnect
Humans with Mother Nature

Immersive story worlds and place-based ritual restore the sense of nature as partner rather than backdrop or resource.

Reconnect
Technologies with Wisdom

AI-generated reflections and biofeedback become tools for deeper presence — technology in service of human flourishing.

These experiences are not fixed scripts. They are living containers: structured enough to build trust and meaning, but open enough for emergence, improvisation, and authentic human interaction.

Participants do not simply consume experiences. They inhabit them, shape them, and carry what they discover back into their lives and communities.

First Portal · 2026

Fuji
Sanctuary

A place devoted to peace, prayer, and cross-cultural harmony

The first Playscape Portal is being developed in collaboration with Fuji Sanctuary at the foot of Mount Fuji — where people of many faiths, nations, and backgrounds gather in prayer for peace and harmony.

What becomes possible when creativity, intelligence, technology, and human attention are gathered not toward destruction, but toward healing, connection, and creation?

Hiroshima was bombed on August 6, 1945, and Nagasaki on August 9. A thousand moons later, this first prototype asks a different question.

Why Fuji Sanctuary
Earth rising toward sky

Mount Fuji gives the work an axis: stillness holding power, beauty asking for humility.

Values already alive

Peace practice, ritual, collective prayer, cross-cultural connection, reverence for nature, and the belief that humanity carries a "divine spark" capable of transformation.

A modern caravanserai

Artists, technologists, spiritual practitioners, and storytellers from different cultures meet in a shared emergent symbolic field.

Physical & virtual

Some gather at the foot of Mount Fuji. Others join virtually from around the world. Together, they participate in a living story about peace, responsibility, memory, and collective possibility.

The Problem It Addresses

Modern life fragments
human experience across
four major divides:

Mind
/ Body

The body carries stress, intuition, emotion, and wisdom that often go unheard.

Individual
/ Community

Isolation, low trust, polarization, and performance culture weaken our ability to collaborate meaningfully.

Civilization
/ Nature

Nature is often experienced as backdrop or resource rather than a stabilizing, grounded relationship.

Machine
/ Human

Technology increasingly shapes attention, emotion, and behavior faster than wisdom can guide it.

Playscape Portals creates environments where groups can practice reconnecting across these divides together.

The Three Intelligences

The Portal Weaves
Three Intelligences

Human Intelligence
Meaning
Participation

Collective participation, performance, and storytelling generate empathy, social learning, and shared meaning.

Nature Intelligence
Perception
Felt truth

Biofeedback — breath, nervous-system awareness, and emotional response — grounds the experience in felt reality and relationship with the living world.

At Fuji Sanctuary, the land itself becomes part of the experience. Participants move through sacred natural spaces while reflecting on their relationship to peace, memory, and the living Earth.

Artificial Intelligence
Reflection
Pattern mirror

AI-generated story reflections help participants recognize patterns, tensions, emotional dynamics, and possibilities emerging within the group experience.

Rather than replacing human wisdom, AI functions as a reflective mirror supporting awareness, dialogue, and collective sense-making.

In a time when AI is often associated with distraction, isolation, and disembodiment, Playscape Portals explores how technology might instead deepen human reflection, empathy, and connection.

What Participants Actually Experience

A Shared
Portal Journey

Participants move through a shared portal journey together — seven moments of increasing depth, presence, and collective meaning.

1
Enter a Portal

Arrive at a physical location at Fuji Sanctuary woven with an immersive virtual layer connecting online participants worldwide.

2
Set Intention

The group begins with a shared question, value, or collective need connected to peace, relationship, memory, or human responsibility.

3
Sense the Field

Become more aware of selves, one another, and the emotional atmosphere through presence, interaction, ritual, and biofeedback awareness.

4
Receive a Story Mirror

AI-generated reflections translate group patterns into symbolic stories, imagery, and narrative meaning.

5
Perform & Rehearse

Take roles, make collaborative choices, improvise, and act together inside a living story environment inspired by mythic journeys and symbolic transformation.

6
Ritualize the Shift

A symbolic gesture, vow, offering, or collective peace action transforms insight into embodied memory and commitment.

7
Carry It Forward

Leave with reflections, relationships, practices, and commitments to bring back into daily life and community.

A Decade of Research
Into Participatory Worlds

For more than a decade, Playscape Portals has evolved by Radiant IRIS through live experiments in immersive storytelling, ritual design, participatory systems, collective sensemaking, and symbolic world-building.

Today, it emerges as a new form of hi-tech make-believe: a participatory medium for practicing how we relate — to ourselves, to one another, to technology, to nature, and to the futures we are creating together.

Our Team of Dreamers and Doers

Our team brings together a unique constellation of artists, storytellers, and cultural innovators dedicated to creating transformative, participatory experiences at the intersection of ritual, technology, and art.

Radiant IRIS

Krishnan Unnikrishnan

A multidisciplinary artist and cultural technologist who designs participatory rituals that remix indigenous oral traditions with modern technologies such as XR and generative AI. His work awakens collective imagination and devotional intelligence through embodied storytelling and collaborative ritual-making.

For over a decade, he has been studying Vedic spiritual philosophy and yogic wisdom with Swami Vidyadhishananda, the inspiration and foundation for his work and public offerings.

Maxwell Kangkolo

An award-winning brand strategist and narrative designer who has led storytelling campaigns for global organizations including Adidas, National Geographic, BMW, and the World Bank.

He works at the intersection at education and media bringing insight and expertise to individuals and organizations in crafting narratives and cultures that connect communities, elevate purpose, and inspire collective action.

Anastasia Nesterova

A creative producer and multimedia director whose award-winning projects span film, theatre, opera, museums, and public art.

Her interdisciplinary practice merges augmented reality, immersive installations, and site-specific performance to create transformative spaces where digital and physical realities and rituals converge.

Stephanie Benedetto

A coach, storyteller, and community catalyst with over 20 years of experience in entrepreneurship, marketing, and conscious business design.

Through The Awakened Business, she crafts playful, story-driven experiences that inspire deep connection, healing, and joyful collaboration among communities worldwide.

Anastasia Akterskaya

An experience designer, theatre director, and storyworld architect working at the intersection of immersive art, ritual design, and collective transformation.

Her practice explores how narrative, space, embodiment, and participatory systems can create new forms of human connection and shared meaning.
