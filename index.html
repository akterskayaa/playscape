02 · What It Is

A Living
Platform

A live physical-digital interactive platform to uncover relational harmony that transforms our shared reality through hi-tech make-believe, rooted in indigenous wisdom.

Using immersive story worlds, biofeedback technology, AI-generated reflections, and collective ritual, participants transform stories from something they consume into something they inhabit and shape together.

Shared Meaning
Immersive story worlds

Living symbolic environments where groups rehearse future scenarios, explore cultural memory, and engage mythic wisdom together.

Felt Truth

Pattern Recognition
AI-generated reflections

The emerging social field translated into narrative sense-making — helping groups see what is happening between them.

Symbolic Coherence
Collective ritual

A shared arc of intention, symbol, and commitment links people to one another, to place, and to a larger order of meaning.

6

First Launch · 2026

A sacred field for the first vibration

The first Playscape Portal is being developed with Fuji Sanctuary at the foot of Mount Fuji — a place devoted to peace, prayer, and cross-cultural harmony.

2026 marks the thousandth moon since Hiroshima and Nagasaki: a symbolic return — a moment to gather creativity, intelligence, technology, and human attention toward creation rather than destruction.

11 · Looking Back

A Decade of Research
Into Participatory Worlds

For more than a decade, Playscape Portals has evolved by Radiant IRIS through live experiments in immersive storytelling, ritual design, participatory systems, collective sensemaking, and symbolic world-building.

Today, it emerges as a new form of hi-tech make-believe: a participatory medium for practicing how we relate — to ourselves, to one another, to technology, to nature, and to the futures we are creating together.
