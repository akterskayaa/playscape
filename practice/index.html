Story as Medicine

We craft immersive storytelling rituals that give our inner worlds outer form — through myth, play, art, and technology.

The invisible made visible

Stories are not
simply things
we tell.

They shape how we perceive ourselves, each other, and the world around us. They influence what we pay attention to, what we value, and what futures we believe are possible.

Every culture emerges through stories.
Every transformation begins when a story changes.

Story as Medicine is our practice of making these invisible narratives visible, tangible, and participatory.

Stories are not explained.
They are experienced.

Enchantment, not escape

Fantasy as escape from reality? We use imagination to enter reality more fully. To deepen relationship with ourselves, each other, and Mother Earth.

People do not arrive to receive a message. They arrive as co-creators inside a living story. The experience itself becomes a cultural commons: a temporary space where new relationships, meanings, and futures can emerge.

Cultural commons

A federation of possible futures

Radiant IRIS is cultivating a growing ecosystem of cultural commons around the world. Each portal, gathering, installation, and experience becomes part of a larger field where communities can remember, reimagine, and recompose who they are.

Together, these experiences form a federation of cultural commons: places where possible futures are not predicted.

Where we are going
