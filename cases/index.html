Radiant Iris / Cases

From Experiments
to Living Experience

A lineage of participatory storyworlds — each one a proof of concept for what comes next.

2017

Playscape

The founding experiment — a fully immersive storyworld where participants became agents inside a living narrative. The first proof that participatory story environments could create genuine collective transformation.

2023

South of Light

An intimate journey into shadow, memory, and collective dreaming. South of Light deepened the methodology around liminal states and explored how space itself becomes a story container that holds and shapes the experience of those within it.

2024

Inloco Metaverse

The first integration of physical and digital storyworlds — exploring how virtual space can hold sacred experience, and how AI can serve as a narrative companion. A bridge between ancestral and emergent technologies.

View the project
2026

WXO StoryField Mirror

Commissioned by the World Experience Organization — a real-time AI narrative installation that reads collective emotional patterns and reflects them back as evolving story.
