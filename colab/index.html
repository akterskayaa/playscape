A creative laboratory with a real-world purpose

Bringing a peace experience to Fuji Sanctuary

No experience with technology is required.

Curiosity, creativity, and a willingness to collaborate are enough.

This October, a prototype experience will be presented at Fuji Sanctuary in Japan — a place where people from many nations, cultures, and traditions gather in support of peace.

Collaboratorium is the creative laboratory helping bring that experience to life.

Participants will work in small interdisciplinary groups to explore ideas, create simple prototypes, test them, and share what they learn.

Why Fuji Sanctuary?

A peace sanctuary at the foot of Mount Fuji

Fuji Sanctuary is a peace sanctuary at the foot of Mount Fuji in Japan.

For decades, people from many nations, cultures, and faiths have gathered there in support of peace and human connection.

This year's Collaboratorium is helping explore how that spirit might be shared with a new generation through storytelling, participation, and technology.
